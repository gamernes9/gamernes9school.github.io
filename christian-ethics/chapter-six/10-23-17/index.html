10-23-17 - Chapter 6

The Image of God

We are created in God's image and have the capacity to relate to God on a personal level and to each other.

We are unique in this and gives us value. We should not murder other humans.

This is foundation of the sanctity of human life. God reaffirmed our sanctity through His Son.

Old Testament Texts

In the context of laws given through Moses about several immoral practices, God warned his people, "You shall not give any of your offspring to offer them to Molech" (Lev. 18:21)

Many nations surround Israel practiced child sacrifice. The influence of those nations led Solomon to cede to their religious practices, including child sacrifice.

Isaiah, Jeremiah, and Ezekiel condemned hte practice.

The ethics of abortion should be determined in this context. The OT always forbade abortion, child sacrifice, etc.

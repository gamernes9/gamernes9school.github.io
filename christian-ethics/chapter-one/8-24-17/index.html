Christian worldview

What's a "worldview"?

What elements make a Christian worldview?

Barna, in a 2017 survey, defines "biblical worldview" as believing that absolute moral truth exists; the Bible is totally accurate in all of the principals it teaches; Satan is considered to be a real being and not merely symbolic; a person cannot earn their way into Heaven by trying to be good or do good works; Jesus Christ lived a sinless life on earth; and God is the all-knowing, all-powerful creator of the world who still rules the universe today.

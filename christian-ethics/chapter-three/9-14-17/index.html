Methods of doing NT ethics

The entire Bible as a book of laws or commandments for controlling conduct

  • Indeed, Jesus' ethical teaching was given with the force of commandments and not merely suggestions
  • This method's limitation is that it doesn't produce morally committed persons, but individuals who struggle to control the worst of immoral conduct
  • Although this method can help in the area of personal morality, it doesn't give sufficient direction in the area of social morality

9-11-17 Chapter 3

Biblical foundation and orientation

Where does Christian Ethics begin?

With the affirmation about God that "He is there and he is not silent"

  1. Gen. 1:1 begins with this assumption and it's affirmed throughout scripture.
  2. God has revealed himself through his works - including his creation (Psalm 19:1-6; Romans 1:18-23) - "General Revelation"

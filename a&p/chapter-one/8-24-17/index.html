Body Cavities

Thoracic Cavity: lungs and heart

Diaphragm: goes up and down when you breathe; separates thoracic and abdominal cavity

Abdominal Cavity: stomach, liver, intestines, and other organs

Pelvic Cavity: reproductive organs, bladder, and rectum

Cranial cavity: brain

Spinal cavity: vertebrae, spinal cord

Mediastinum: separates the lungs into right and left cavities

Body Quadrants and Regions

Epi: above, over

Hype: below

Hyper: over

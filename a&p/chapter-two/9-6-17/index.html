Valence: refers to the outer shell of an atom

The rule of eights: atoms will always try to have eight valence electrons

Ion: have a charge

  • Anion: negative charge
  • Cation: positive charge

Types of Chemical Bonds

    Ionic bonds: form when electrons are completely transferred from one atom to another

    Covalent bonds: molecules in which atoms share electrons

    Hydrogen bonds: when a hydrogen atom forms a bridge between an oxygen atom and another atom
